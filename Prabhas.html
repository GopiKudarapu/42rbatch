About Prabhas

**Prabhas** (born 23 Oct 1979) is a leading Indian actor in Telugu cinema, known as the *Rebel Star*. He rose to fame with *Varsham* (2004) and gained pan-India stardom with the *Baahubali* series, with *Baahubali 2* becoming the first Indian film to gross over ₹1000 crore worldwide. His other hits include *Chatrapathi*, *Darling*, *Mirchi* (Nandi Award), *Saaho*, *Salaar*, and *Kalki 2898 AD*. Prabhas is the only Indian actor with six films grossing over ₹100 crore on opening and in the Hindi market. He's also the first South Indian actor with a wax statue at Madame Tussauds. He has received 7 Filmfare nominations, 1 Nandi Award, and 1 SIIMA Award.
